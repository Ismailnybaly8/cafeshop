Our Story

Founded in 2018, The Cozy Cup began as a dream to create a warm, welcoming space where the community could gather over exceptional coffee. Our passionate baristas source the finest beans from around the world and roast them to perfection.

We believe in supporting local farmers and sustainable practices. Every cup you enjoy helps build a better future for coffee communities worldwide.
